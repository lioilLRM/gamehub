About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game that puts your skills to the ultimate test. Face waves of increasingly challenging monsters while developing your character's abilities and discovering powerful combinations of skills.

With its roguelike elements and permanent progression system, each playthrough offers a unique experience. Can you survive long enough to become the ultimate monster slayer?
